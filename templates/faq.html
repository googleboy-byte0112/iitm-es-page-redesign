Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the IIT Madras BS in Electronic Systems program.

General Questions

What is BS in Electronic Systems?

BS in Electronic Systems is a four-year online degree program offered by IIT Madras. The program focuses on electronics, embedded programming, digital systems, and control engineering. Students can learn at their own pace and schedule while receiving a high-quality education from one of India's premier institutions.

Is this an official degree from IIT Madras?

Yes, this is an official degree approved by the IIT Madras senate. Upon completion, you will receive a BS degree from IIT Madras and become an alumnus of the institute.

Eligibility & Application

Who can apply for this program?

Anyone who has passed Class 12 (or equivalent) with Physics and Mathematics can apply, regardless of their age or background. Additionally, students who have appeared for their Class 11 final exams (with Physics and Mathematics) can also apply, and if qualified, can join after passing Class 12.

What is the application process?

The application process involves:

  1. Submitting an online application
  2. Completing a 4-week qualifier course
  3. Taking the qualifier exam
  4. If qualified, enrolling in the program

Program Structure

What is the program structure?

The program is divided into three levels:

  • Foundation Level (44 credits)
  • Diploma Level (42 credits)
  • BS Degree Level (56 credits)

Total: 142 credits for BS Degree

How long does it take to complete the program?

The program is flexible and can be completed at your own pace. Full-time students typically take 4 courses per term, while working professionals are recommended to take 2-3 courses per term. The program can be completed in 4 years, but you have the flexibility to take longer if needed.

Fees & Payment

What are the program fees?

The fee structure is designed to be affordable:

  • Application Fee: ₹3,000
  • Qualifier Fee: ₹5,000
  • Course Fee: Approximately ₹2,000 per credit

Scholarships are available for eligible candidates.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, scholarships are available for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The scholarship details and application process will be shared with eligible candidates after admission.

Exams & Assessment

How are the exams conducted?

While the coursework is online, exams are conducted in-person at designated exam centers across India. The assessment includes:

  • Weekly assignments (online)
  • Mid-term exams (in-person)
  • End-term exams (in-person)
  • Lab courses (in-person at IIT Madras campus)

Where are the exam centers located?

Exam centers are available in major cities across India. The exact list of cities is provided before each examination cycle, and students can choose their preferred center based on availability.
