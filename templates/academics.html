Academic Structure

A comprehensive guide to the BS in Electronic Systems program

Overall Structure

The program comprises three levels and a learner has to successfully complete all three levels to get the BS Degree from IIT Madras.

There is flexibility to exit at any level. Depending on the courses completed and credits earned, the learner can receive:

Foundation Level

43 Credits
  • 9 Theory courses
  • 5 Labs
  • Exit with: Foundation Certificate
Level 1 of 3

Diploma Level

43 Credits
  • 9 Theory courses
  • 3 Labs
  • 1 Project course
  • Exit with: Diploma
Level 2 of 3

BS Degree Level

56 Credits
  • 12 Theory courses
  • 1 Lab
  • Optional Apprenticeship
  • Exit with: BS Degree
Level 3 of 3
142 Total Credits
